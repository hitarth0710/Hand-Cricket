Welcome to Hand Cricket

Play the classic Hand Cricket game using computer vision!

How to Play:

  1. Choose to bat or bowl first
  2. Show your hand gesture (0-5 fingers) to the camera
  3. If batting, your fingers count as runs unless they match the AI's number
  4. If bowling, try to match the AI's number to get a wicket
  5. Each team has 5 wickets
  6. After a team loses all wickets, the other team tries to beat their score
